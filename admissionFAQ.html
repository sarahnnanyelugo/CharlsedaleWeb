Frequently asked Questions

Where is the School located?

The School is located at No. 12 Army Range Road, Off Eneka-Igwuruta Road, Igwuruta, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Click this link to view location https://maps.app.goo.gl/4hEwR9zqck8mwfq89

Is CDMIS a boarding or a day school?

CDMIS is a full boarding secondary school.

What is the requirement for admission?

The child should be 10years by September of the admission year and must have completed primary education. The child is also expected to take and pass the Entrance Examination.

When is the Entrance Examination?

The Scholarship Entrance and the Supplementary Entrance examinations take place in January and February respectively.

How can I apply?

Click on this link to apply. https://charlesdale.educare.school/admission-form

What curriculum does your school offer?

We offer the Nigerian, British and Canadian Curricula.

Are fees discounted for parents with more than one child admitted in the school?

Does CDMIS admist students into Senior Secondary School?(Year 10)

Yes we do.

What strategies have been put in place by the school to cope with the trending covid-19 pandemic?

All specified covid-19 protocol is duely and strictly observed. We also have stand by mdeicial doctors for related emergency

Which International Examination is offered in CDMIS?

The International Examinations we prepare our students for are; IGCSE, Secondary Checkpoint, SAT, IELTS, Kangarou Sans Frontiers, TOEFL, ACTS and OSSD Certificate Examinations
